# Contractor and Contributor Intellectual Property Agreement

This Contractor and Contributor Intellectual Property Agreement (the “Agreement”) is entered into as of **[Effective Date]**, by and between **[Company Name], LLC**, a Michigan limited liability company (the “Company”), and **[Contractor Name]** (“Contributor”).

## 1. Purpose

The Company engages Contributor to provide services, content, designs, documents, software, media, research, field plans, educational materials, or other work product. This Agreement clarifies ownership, confidentiality, and permitted use of that work.

## 2. Relationship

Contributor is engaged as an independent contractor unless a separate written employment agreement states otherwise. Nothing in this Agreement creates any promise of employment, partnership, joint venture, or ownership in the Company.

## 3. Definitions

**Work Product** means all deliverables, drafts, inventions, discoveries, designs, diagrams, software, templates, photographs, writing, audio, video, data, analyses, mapping assets, branding materials, and other materials created, conceived, reduced to practice, or delivered by Contributor in connection with services for the Company.

**Pre-Existing Materials** means materials owned or controlled by Contributor before the effective date or developed independently outside the scope of Company work and expressly listed in Exhibit A.

## 4. Assignment of rights

To the fullest extent permitted by law, Contributor hereby assigns to the Company all right, title, and interest in and to the Work Product and all related intellectual property rights, excluding only the Pre-Existing Materials listed in Exhibit A.

## 5. Further assurances

Contributor shall sign reasonable documents and provide reasonable cooperation to help the Company confirm, register, enforce, or defend its rights in the Work Product.

## 6. License back for Pre-Existing Materials

If Contributor includes approved Pre-Existing Materials in any deliverable, Contributor grants the Company a perpetual, worldwide, nonexclusive, royalty-free license to use, reproduce, modify, display, distribute, and sublicense those materials solely as needed to use the deliverable.

## 7. Moral rights and waivers

To the extent permitted by law, Contributor waives any moral rights or similar rights that would interfere with the Company's use of the Work Product.

## 8. Confidentiality

Contributor shall not use or disclose the Company's confidential information except as needed to perform authorized services. This obligation survives termination.

## 9. Open-source and third-party materials

Contributor shall not incorporate open-source, stock, or third-party materials into deliverables unless Contributor has disclosed them in writing and the Company has approved the license terms.

## 10. No conflicting obligations

Contributor represents that entering into this Agreement and performing services will not violate any other agreement or legal duty.

## 11. Return of materials

Upon request or termination, Contributor shall promptly return or destroy confidential information and Company property, subject to reasonable archival copies required by law.

## 12. Compensation

Contributor's compensation, if any, is governed by a separate services agreement, statement of work, invoice approval, or other written arrangement.

## 13. Injunctive relief

Contributor acknowledges that breaches involving IP or confidentiality may cause irreparable harm and that the Company may seek injunctive relief in addition to other remedies.

## 14. Governing law

This Agreement is governed by Michigan law, without regard to conflict-of-law rules.

## 15. Entire agreement

This Agreement, together with any statement of work and Exhibit A, is the entire understanding on these IP and confidentiality matters unless supplemented in writing.
