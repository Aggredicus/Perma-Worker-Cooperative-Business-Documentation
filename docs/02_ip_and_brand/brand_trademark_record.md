## Brand usage rules

1. The legal owner of the core brand assets should be the Company unless a written license says otherwise.
2. Brand use by contractors, collaborators, licensees, and affiliates must follow written permission.
3. Any new logo variation, course brand, product line, or sub-brand should be entered here before public release.
4. Public claims that a mark is registered should only be made if registration has actually issued in the relevant jurisdiction.
5. Archive earlier versions of logos and names rather than overwriting history.
