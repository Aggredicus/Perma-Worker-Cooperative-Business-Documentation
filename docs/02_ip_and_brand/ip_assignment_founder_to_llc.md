# Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement (Founder to LLC)

This Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement (the “Agreement”) is made effective as of **[Effective Date]**, by and between **[Founder Name]** (“Assignor”) and **[Company Name], LLC**, a Michigan limited liability company (the “Company”).

## 1. Purpose

Assignor has created or controlled certain intellectual property useful to the Company's business. The parties wish to transfer ownership of the assigned assets to the Company so the Company can operate, license, develop, and protect them.

## 2. Assigned assets

Assignor hereby sells, assigns, transfers, and conveys to the Company all right, title, and interest in the assets listed on **Exhibit A**, together with associated goodwill and all related intellectual property rights, including copyrights, trademark rights, trade secrets, know-how, design rights, and other proprietary rights to the extent applicable.

## 3. Excluded assets

Assets listed on **Exhibit B** are excluded from this assignment and remain the property of Assignor unless later transferred in writing.

## 4. Consideration

The consideration for this assignment shall be:
- [cash amount],
- and/or a capital contribution credit,
- and/or other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are acknowledged.

## 5. Further assurances

Assignor shall execute additional reasonable documents and provide reasonable cooperation necessary to confirm, perfect, record, or enforce the Company's ownership of the assigned assets.

## 6. Representations of Assignor

Assignor represents, to Assignor's knowledge, that:
1. Assignor has the right to transfer the assigned assets.
2. Assignor has not knowingly granted conflicting rights in the assigned assets except as disclosed in Exhibit C.
3. The assigned assets do not knowingly include third-party materials that cannot legally be transferred except as disclosed in Exhibit C.

## 7. No obligation to exploit

The Company has no obligation to commercialize, register, use, or maintain any assigned asset.

## 8. Governing law

This Agreement is governed by Michigan law.

## 9. Entire agreement

This Agreement and its exhibits are the complete understanding regarding the transfer of the assigned assets.
