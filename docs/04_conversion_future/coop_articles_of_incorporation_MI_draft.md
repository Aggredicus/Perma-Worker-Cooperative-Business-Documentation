> This is a future-state drafting worksheet, not a filing-ready instrument. Michigan cooperative and employee-owned structures can require careful statute selection and conversion planning. Use this draft only with Michigan counsel when conversion is active.

## Draft structural points to resolve with counsel

1. Which Michigan statute and entity form best matches the intended worker-owned model.
2. Whether the conversion should use a cooperative corporation, employee-owned corporation, or another permitted form.
3. How existing LLC membership interests convert into shares, membership units, or another ownership class.
4. Whether investor or preferred interests will exist alongside worker-owner interests.
5. Whether any public-benefit or special purpose language is strategically useful.
