# Glossary

## Core entity terms

**Company** — The legal business entity operating under this documentation suite.

**Founder** — The initial organizer, member, or manager who creates the Company and may contribute capital, intellectual property, labor, and strategic direction.

**Member** — A person or entity holding a membership interest in the LLC under the operating agreement.

**Manager** — A person designated to manage the LLC if the entity is manager-managed.

**Worker-Owner** — A worker who has been admitted to ownership under the future cooperative structure or other approved worker-ownership framework.

**Board** — The governing board of the future cooperative, if adopted.

## Financial terms

**Capital Contribution** — Cash, property, services, or enforceable promises contributed to the Company subject to the governing documents and accounting treatment.

**Capital Account** — A bookkeeping account tracking a member's economic interest consistent with the tax and accounting method used.

**Draw** — A non-payroll owner distribution or withdrawal, if permitted.

**Guaranteed Payment** — A payment to a member treated according to the tax classification of the entity and subject to professional tax review.

**Patronage** — The value of a worker-owner's labor or participation used in a cooperative allocation framework.

**Patronage Dividend / Allocation** — A distribution or allocation based on patronage rather than pure invested capital, subject to cooperative law and tax treatment.

## Governance terms

**Operating Agreement** — The LLC's governing contract among members and the Company.

**Written Consent** — A written approval used instead of a meeting when permitted.

**Quorum** — The minimum participation required to conduct valid business under the governing rules.

**Supermajority** — A voting threshold greater than a simple majority.

**Reserved Matter** — A decision requiring heightened approval because of its significance.

## Legal and IP terms

**Work Product** — Any deliverable, writing, design, software, template, file, image, process, training content, or other output created in connection with Company work.

**Pre-Existing IP** — Intellectual property developed before a relationship with the Company or explicitly excluded from assignment.

**Assigned IP** — Intellectual property transferred to the Company by written agreement.

**Licensed IP** — Intellectual property the Company uses under permission without owning it.

**Confidential Information** — Nonpublic business, client, financial, operational, or technical information that the Company designates or reasonably treats as confidential.

## Operations terms

**Client Work** — Custom services or deliverables created for a customer.

**Digital Product** — A non-physical product such as a template, course, file, software tool, or download.

**Conversion** — The future legal or operational transition from founder-led LLC structure to worker cooperative or similar employee-owned form.

**Adoption Date** — The date a document or policy is approved.

**Effective Date** — The date a document or policy begins governing conduct.
