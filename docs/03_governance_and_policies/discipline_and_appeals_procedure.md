# Discipline and Appeals Procedure

## Purpose

This procedure provides a fair internal process for warnings, probation, suspension, and expulsion recommendations.

## Progressive discipline ladder

1. Coaching or verbal counseling
2. Written warning
3. Final warning or probation
4. Suspension
5. Expulsion recommendation

## Suspension

Suspension requires a two-thirds vote of the full board and written notice stating duration, conditions, and appeal rights.

## Expulsion procedure

1. Written allegations are delivered.
2. The member receives relevant evidence summary.
3. The member may submit a written response.
4. A hearing is held.
5. The board issues a written recommendation.
6. The member may appeal within 10 calendar days.
7. After appeal completion or waiver, the members may vote.
8. Expulsion requires a two-thirds member vote.

## Appeal body

Unless otherwise adopted, appeals should be heard by an impartial panel designated by the board that excludes conflicted persons, or by an outside facilitator if internal neutrality is not credible.
