# Election and Voting Procedure

## Purpose

This procedure standardizes nominations, ballots, quorum verification, and vote counting.

## Nominations

Candidates may be nominated by the Governance Committee, by petition, or from the floor if the meeting notice permits.

## Secret ballots

Secret ballots should be used for board elections, membership admissions if requested, and expulsion votes.

## Quorum verification

The Secretary shall confirm quorum before any vote requiring member action.

## Counting

Vote counts should be recorded in a way that preserves ballot confidentiality while allowing audit of totals.

## Tie handling

If a board election tie occurs, the tied candidates proceed to a runoff unless the election rules provide another neutral method.
