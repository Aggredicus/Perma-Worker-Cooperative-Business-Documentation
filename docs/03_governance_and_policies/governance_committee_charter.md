# Governance Committee Charter

## Purpose

The Governance Committee supports board integrity, director accountability, policy coherence, and healthy cooperative process.

## Responsibilities

The committee should:
- review board process quality and bylaw compliance,
- recommend improvements to governance documents,
- review concerns about director conduct or role confusion,
- recommend whether a Worker Director removal-for-cause process should be placed before the members,
- support founder-sunset readiness planning,
- help identify governance training needs.

## Limits

The committee does not replace the board or membership voting powers. It may recommend actions, but removals, elections, bylaw amendments, and other constitutional decisions remain where the bylaws place them.

## Composition

The board should appoint at least two non-conflicted persons to this committee when scale permits. Before a formal committee exists, the board may designate an ad hoc governance review panel or outside facilitator for a specific issue.
