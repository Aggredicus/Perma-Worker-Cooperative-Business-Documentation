# Finance Committee Charter

## Purpose

The Finance Committee supports the board’s oversight of budgets, reserve targets, patronage calculations, and financial policy integrity.

## Responsibilities

The committee shall:
- review monthly or quarterly financial statements,
- evaluate reserve sufficiency,
- review any proposed patronage declaration,
- check consistency of compensation categories used for patronage,
- review extraordinary debt proposals,
- recommend financial controls improvements.

## Patronage review role

Before any patronage declaration, the committee should review:
- net surplus determination,
- reserve floor status,
- total eligible labor compensation,
- member-level calculation worksheets,
- tax-preparer notes,
- proposed cash versus retained portions.

## Reporting

The committee should issue a concise written recommendation to the board before annual patronage decisions.
