# Director and Governance Code of Conduct

## Standard

Directors and governance officers are expected to act with care, loyalty, confidentiality, candor, and respect for process.

## Duties

Each director should:
- prepare for meetings,
- read materials reasonably in advance,
- ask honest questions,
- disclose conflicts,
- protect confidential information,
- avoid retaliation,
- distinguish strategic governance from personal grievance,
- respect the difference between founder transition protections and indefinite founder dominance.

## Prohibited conduct

- using office for personal enrichment without disclosure,
- disclosing confidential governance information improperly,
- retaliating against members for lawful dissent,
- obstructing quorum or process in bad faith,
- discriminatory or harassing behavior.
