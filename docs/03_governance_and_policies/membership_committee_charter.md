# Membership Committee Charter

## Purpose

The Membership Committee supports fair, documented, and consistent worker-owner admissions, orientation, and membership development.

## Responsibilities

The committee shall:
- review candidacy progress,
- apply the membership rubric consistently,
- interview candidates when appropriate,
- issue written recommendations to the board,
- support governance onboarding,
- monitor whether candidacy is being used fairly rather than as indefinite limbo.

## Membership admission rubric categories

The committee should consider:
- reliability and attendance,
- work quality,
- safety compliance,
- collaborative conduct,
- governance readiness,
- mission alignment,
- confidentiality and judgment,
- willingness to participate in shared ownership responsibly.

## Outputs

For each candidate, the committee should produce a short written recommendation stating:
- recommended,
- recommended with conditions,
- not recommended,
- reasons and any remediation steps.

## Appeals interface

The committee may assist in factual review for membership denials if the board requests it, but final board and member voting authority remains as set out in the bylaws.
