# Initial Capitalization Table

## Purpose

This document records the founding economic structure of the LLC at launch. It should be used together with the operating agreement and the accounting records. It does not replace tax capital accounts or legal schedules required by counsel.

## Capitalization principles

1. No ownership rights are recognized unless documented in writing.
2. Contributions must be valued and recorded consistently.
3. Sweat equity, future work promises, and reimbursement claims should not be treated casually; each requires explicit written treatment.
4. This table must remain aligned with the operating agreement.

## Equity controls

The following actions require written approval and an update to this document:
- issuance of additional membership interests,
- admission of a new member,
- repurchase or forfeiture of an interest,
- recognition of material non-cash contributions,
- conversion of debt into equity-like interests,
- adoption of any worker-owner admission economics.
