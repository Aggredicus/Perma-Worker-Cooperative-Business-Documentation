# EIN and Tax Setup Checklist

## Purpose

This checklist organizes the federal, state, payroll, and bookkeeping setup steps that should follow entity formation.

> The IRS instructs businesses to form the entity first and then apply for an EIN. Review current IRS guidance before application and before selecting any tax classification. 

## 1. Entity prerequisites

- [ ] Filed Michigan Articles of Organization
- [ ] State filing confirmation received
- [ ] Legal business name finalized
- [ ] Responsible party identified for EIN application
- [ ] Registered address and mailing address confirmed

### IRS application preparation fields
- Legal name of entity
- Trade name / DBA if any
- Mailing address
- County and state of principal business
- Responsible party SSN/ITIN/EIN
- Number of members
- Date business started or acquired
- Closing month of accounting year
- Highest number of expected employees in the next 12 months
- Principal activity and product/service description

## 3. Federal tax decisions

Review with CPA or EA:
- [ ] Default tax classification understood
- [ ] S election considered, rejected, or approved
- [ ] Reasonable compensation implications reviewed if S election is considered
- [ ] Estimated tax process established
- [ ] Annual return type identified
- [ ] Bookkeeping method chosen (cash or accrual)

## 4. State and local setup

- [ ] Michigan tax registrations reviewed
- [ ] Sales/use tax applicability reviewed for digital and physical sales
- [ ] Payroll withholding and unemployment accounts reviewed if hiring workers
- [ ] Local permits or contractor registrations reviewed where work is performed
- [ ] Business insurance binder obtained

## 5. Payroll and workforce setup

If hiring workers:
- [ ] Workers classified correctly as employees or contractors
- [ ] I-9 and W-4 processes prepared
- [ ] New hire reporting obligations reviewed
- [ ] Payroll system selected
- [ ] Workers' compensation requirements reviewed
- [ ] Employee handbook/onboarding plan created

## 6. Banking and accounting

- [ ] Business checking account opened
- [ ] Business savings / tax reserve account opened
- [ ] Payment processor connected to company legal entity
- [ ] Chart of accounts established
- [ ] Owner contribution and reimbursement workflows defined
- [ ] Receipt retention procedure established
- [ ] Monthly close process assigned
